Understanding Return of Capital (ROC)

Return of Capital is not immediately taxable, but it has important implications:

  • ROC reduces your cost basis in the ETF shares
  • When you sell, you'll pay capital gains tax on the reduced basis
  • If your basis reaches $0, additional ROC becomes taxable
  • Keep detailed records of all ROC distributions
