3. Specific Risks of YieldMax ETFs

🔻 NAV Decay Risk

YieldMax ETFs can experience significant NAV (Net Asset Value) decay over time, meaning the fund's share price may decline even while paying distributions.

⛔ Capped Upside

The covered call strategy limits potential gains. You will not fully participate in the underlying stock's price appreciation.

📉 Full Downside Exposure

While upside is capped, you bear the full downside risk if the underlying stock declines in value.

💸 Distribution Variability

Monthly distributions can vary significantly and may be $0 in some months. High yields are not guaranteed.

📊 Return of Capital

A significant portion of distributions may be return of capital, not income. This reduces your cost basis and may result in higher taxes upon sale.

🎲 Volatility Risk

These ETFs invest in highly volatile stocks. Price swings can be extreme and rapid.
