Troubleshooting Common Issues

If prices aren't updating:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Clear your browser cache (Ctrl+F5)
  • Disable ad blockers that might interfere
  • Try a different browser

Login issues can be resolved by:

  • Resetting your password
  • Checking CAPS LOCK is off
  • Clearing cookies for our site
  • Contacting support if problems persist

Ensure accurate calculations by:

  • Using current yield data
  • Checking input values for errors
  • Understanding yield vs distribution rate
  • Accounting for fees and taxes
