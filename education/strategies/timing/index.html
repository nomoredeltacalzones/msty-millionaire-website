Technical Indicators That Actually Work

1. Implied Volatility Rank (IVR)

Sweet Spot: 30-70th percentile

Measures current IV relative to 52-week range. Above 70th percentile signals potential selling opportunity.

2. Distribution Coverage Ratio

Warning Level: Below 0.8x

When option premiums can't cover distributions, NAV decay accelerates. Critical timing signal.

3. Underlying Asset Momentum

Bullish Signal: 20-day MA > 50-day MA

Strong underlying momentum reduces call exercise risk and improves distribution sustainability.

4. Options Skew Analysis

Optimal Range: 10-20% skew

Measures put vs call premium differential. High skew indicates fear‚Äîoften a buying opportunity.
